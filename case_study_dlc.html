DLC Website Redesign

UX Design / UI Design / Information Architecture

CLIENT:

Duquesne Light Company (DLC)

Team

Gatesman+Dave

Date

Fall 2015 - Spring 2016

Website

www.duquesnelight.com

Project Description

Gatesman, formally known as Gatesman+Dave, was tasked with redesigning Duquesne Light Company’s (DLC) website.

My role, as the User Experience (UX) Designer, was to evaluate the current site, identify the needs of the target audiences and help outline the basic structure and layout of the new site using a sitemap and wireframes.

Website prior to redesign

Research and Discovery

During this phase, I presented a usability audit to DLC that assessed the quality and effectiveness of their user interface. This evaluation was based off of industry best practices, research gathered from a competitive review, as well as insight gained from a small focus group of DLC customers.

Using this research, I was able to identify the main target audiences and create user personas to help capture and prioritize the main goals during the rebuild process.

User persona example

UX and UI Design

Using Axure, I created a proposed sitemap that focused on restructuring the navigation to help eliminate the duplicated pages and hidden content to make it easier overall for the users to find the information they need. Another priority was also to reduce the number of clicks users would take to pay their bill online. In total, the proposed sitemap consisted of 140+ pages.

Proposed sitemap example

For the wireframes, I created templates for the main layouts with notes explaining the basic functionality of the page elements. These deliverables acted as references for use in design and development. I continued to provide UX direction throughout the digital process.

Wireframes - My Account example (public and logged in view)

Results

The new DLC website launched in Spring 2016.

Though Gatesman was not able to partake in the surveys sent out to analyze the site success, we did find some user feedback on Reddit.

“I normally don't have anything nice to say about any service company, but I went to Duquesne Light website to pay my bill and it was completely redone and had a giant yellow box that said "online account login." Also scroll down and it has big buttons for the most common things you would do on their web page.

I don't know when they did it but kudos to them for doing something that actually was helpful for the customers..."
