About

I have recently been working at Gatesman and before that, I was a graduate from Robert Morris University. My work encompasses a variety of skillsets including UX, IA, user interface design (UI), technical writing, front-end development and graphic design.

Focus

Throughout all of my projects, I focus on understanding the needs of the users and advocating UX best practices whenever possible. I believe that teamwork, accessibility and creativity all play an essential part in the digital process.

Deliverables:

  • Usability Audits
  • Competitive Analyses
  • User Personas
  • User Flow Charts
  • Sitemaps
  • Wireframes / Interactive Prototypes
  • Mockups
